Zihao Fu

University of Cambridge

Zihao Fu (付子豪)

I am a Research Associate (PostDoc) in the Language Technology Lab, University of Cambridge. My research mainly focuses on Natural Language Processing, Text Generation, Machine Learning, Biomedical Applications, and etc. Before I came to Cambridge, I received my Ph.D. degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Wai Lam. I also have three years experience of developing large-scale distributed parallel algorithms for the PAI plantform in Alibaba Cloud.
