CUHK Hire Postdocs and RAs in LLMs, NLP, Linguistics, and Cognitive Science

The Language Processing Lab at the Department of Linguistics and Modern Languages, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) is now inviting applications for Postdoctoral Researchers and Research Assistants to join our interdisciplinary projects on Large Language Models (LLMs) × Linguistics / Psychology / Neuroscience / AI Ethics.

Our research group is jointly led by scholars active in the fields of Natural Language Processing, Linguistics, Cognitive Science, and AI Ethics. We aim to explore the interpretability mechanisms of large language models, the relationship between language and cognition, and conduct theoretical analysis, system development, and experimental studies in these domains.

Lab Introduction

Zhenguang Cai, Professor at CUHK, PI of the Language Processing Lab. He obtained his PhD in Psychology from the University of Edinburgh and was a recipient of the “Future Research Leaders” award from the Economic and Social Research Council (UK). His research focuses on the cognitive mechanisms of language production and the behavioral and interpretability aspects of language models.

Zihao Fu, Research Assistant Professor at CUHK. He received his PhD from CUHK and previously conducted research on large language models and natural language processing at the Oxford Internet Institute and the Language Technology Lab at the University of Cambridge. His research interests include fine-tuning of large language models, interpretability analysis, and algorithmic fairness.

Preferred Research Background

We welcome applicants from diverse academic backgrounds. Preference will be given to those with one or more of the following experiences:

  • Research experience in Linguistics, Psychology, or Neuroscience;
  • Research experience with large language models (e.g., GPT, BERT, LLaMA), including model deployment, fine-tuning, prompt engineering, and embedding analysis;
  • Solid research skills in Data Science, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, or Natural Language Processing.

Positions and Eligibility

◆ Postdoctoral Researcher (CUHK Shenzhen Research Institute)

  • In good physical health, with a PhD degree obtained within the past three years;
  • Must be fully committed to postdoctoral research work. Sponsored or in-service personnel (e.g., military) must provide documentation proving permission for full-time research;
  • In-service personnel may not conduct postdoctoral research part-time, and the host institution may not recruit its own former employees as postdocs;
  • Preference will be given to applicants who have received doctoral excellence awards, hold authorized invention patents, or have participated in national/provincial major research projects.

◆ Postdoctoral Researcher (CUHK Main Campus)

  • No specific age or employment status restrictions;
  • PhD degree in relevant fields (e.g., NLP, Machine Learning, Linguistics, Cognitive Science);
  • Capable of conducting independent research and willing to participate in interdisciplinary collaboration.

◆ Research Assistant (CUHK Main Campus)

  • Bachelor’s degree or above in a relevant discipline;
  • Familiarity with NLP, Machine Learning, Linguistics, or Cognitive Science methods;
  • Candidates with strong programming, modeling, or experimental design skills will be preferred.

Compensation and Benefits

  • Flexible start date (the sooner, the better);
  • The initial appointment period is one year (reviewed annually) and may be continuously renewed.
  • Salary will follow CUHK standards, with pension and medical insurance benefits provided.

招纳贤士 | 博士后研究员及研究助理招聘

香港中文大学语言学及现代语言系语言处理实验室（Language Processing Lab, Department of Linguistics and Modern Languages, CUHK）现面向全球诚聘博士后研究员与研究助理，加入我们正在开展的多个大语言模型（LLM）× 语言学 / 心理学 / 神经科学 / AI伦理交叉研究项目。

本项目组由多位在自然语言处理、语言学、认知科学及人工智能伦理等领域活跃的学者联合指导，致力于探索大语言模型的可解释性机制、语言与认知之间的关系等领域，并开展相关的理论分析、系统开发与实验验证工作。

实验室介绍

Zhenguang Cai (蔡振光)，香港中文大学教授，语言处理实验室PI。毕业于爱丁堡大学，获得心理学博士学位，曾获英国经济及社会科学委员会“未来研究领军”的项目资助。研究方向为语言产生的认知机制、语言模型的行为机制与可解释性等。

Zihao Fu (付子豪), 香港中文大学研究助理教授。毕业于香港中文大学，曾在牛津大学互联网学院与剑桥大学语言处理实验室从事大语言模型与自然语言处理相关研究。研究方向涵盖大语言模型的微调、可解释性分析以及算法公正性等。

研究背景

我们欢迎来自不同学科背景的申请人，具备以下研究经验之一者将获得优先考虑：
语言学、心理学或神经科学相关研究经验；
大语言模型（如GPT、BERT、Llama等）相关科研经验，包括模型部署、微调、提示词工程、词嵌入分析等；
数据科学、机器学习、深度学习或自然语言处理等领域的扎实研究能力。
